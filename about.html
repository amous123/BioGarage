DNA Revolution

french: DNA Revolution

Since the completion of the human genome project, we have entered an era of unprecedented breakthrough and innovation in Molecular Biology, which has been dubbed “The DNA Revolution”. bioGARAGE builds upon this movement by serving as a Molecular Biology hub embracing the curiosity-driven and pioneering spirit shared by undergraduates, graduate students, faculty, and the biotechnology and pharmaceutical industries in Ottawa, Ontario, Canada, and beyond. It is a hands-on and open learning space, where students can explore and cultivate their initiative and creativity in redesigning and manipulating the building blocks of life. bioGARAGE is a hub for inclusive innovation fostering the entrepreneurial spirit of students while connecting them to local startups, and engaging them in grand challenges for the betterment of society.
